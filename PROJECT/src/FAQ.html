Fashion Wear: Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the Fashion Wear FAQ page! Here, we’ve compiled answers to some of the most common questions our customers have. If you don’t find what you’re
looking for, feel free to contact our customer support team.

1. Orders and Payments

Q: How do I place an order?
A: To place an order, simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase by providing your
shipping details and payment information.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit and cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI, PayPal, and select digital wallets. All payments
are processed securely.

Q: Can I change or cancel my order after it’s been placed?
A: If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Once your order has been processed or shipped, we may
not be able to make changes, but we’ll do our best to assist you.

2. Shipping and Delivery

Q: What are your shipping options?
A: We offer several shipping options to meet your needs, including standard and expedited shipping. Shipping costs and delivery times vary based on your location and
the shipping method selected at checkout.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: Once your order has been shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier’s website.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, Fashion Wear ships to many countries around the world. International shipping costs and delivery times will be calculated at checkout. Please note that customs
fees and import duties may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

3. Returns and Exchanges

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase, provided the items are in their original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for
more details on how to initiate a return.

Q: How do I return an item?
A: To return an item, log in to your account, visit the Order History page, and select the item(s) you wish to return. Follow the instructions to print a return label and send
the package back to us.

Q: Can I exchange an item?
A: Yes, we offer exchanges for different sizes or colors, subject to availability. If you need a different item altogether, please return the original item for a refund and place
a new order.

4. Products and Sizing

Q: How do I find the right size?
A: We offer detailed size guides on each product page to help you choose the right fit. If you’re unsure, our virtual fitting room tool can provide personalized
recommendations based on your measurements.

Q: Are your products sustainably made?
A: Yes, sustainability is a core value at Fashion Wear. We use eco-friendly materials like organic cotton and recycled fabrics, and we follow ethical sourcing practices
to ensure our products are made responsibly.

Q: How do I care for my Fashion Wear clothing?
A: Each product comes with specific care instructions on the label. Generally, we recommend washing in cold water and air drying to preserve the quality and
longevity of your garments.

5. Account and Support

Q: Do I need an account to place an order?
A: No, you can place an order as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track your orders, save your preferences, and access exclusive offers.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: If you’ve forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page, and we’ll send you instructions to reset it.

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team on the website. We’re here to help with any questions or concerns.

6. Promotions and Discounts

Q: How do I use a promo code?
A: Enter your promo code in the designated box at checkout, and the discount will be applied to your order. Please note that only one promo code can be used per
order, and some codes may have restrictions.

Q: Do you offer student or military discounts?
A: Yes, we offer special discounts for students and military personnel. Please verify your status through our partner verification service at checkout to receive your discount.

Q: Can I combine multiple discounts?
A: Unfortunately, multiple discounts cannot be combined. If you have more than one promo code, please choose the one that offers the best value for your order.

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Thank you for choosing Fashion Wear!
